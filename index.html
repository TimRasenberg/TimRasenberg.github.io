Choosing what study I was going to do was actually a pretty difficult decision, I have quite a lot of interests.

I was torn between 3 main options:

  1. Game development in Breda
  2. Film school
  3. HBO ICT

I chose for the third option because I think the study really fits me as a person.

I don't like studying day in and day out, I like to get something done. And in this case that would be in the form of projects.

I went for an HBO level study because I feel like that fits my capabilities best. It pushes me just enough to where I can learn new things

while not overwhelming me.

Even if I was at a crossroads, I feel like I made the right decision.
